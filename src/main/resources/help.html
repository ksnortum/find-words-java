Overview

Top

Scrabble Words will help you find words to play in the game of Scrabble. You enter letters of the tiles you have in your hand. Optionally, you can enter a letter or letters on the board that the suggested word must contain. You can also enter the letters the suggested word must start with and end with. Three dictionaries are supported: The Official Scrabble Players Dictionary, or OSPD; the Official Tournament and Club Word List, or TWL; and SOWPODS, which is used in most countries outside the USA.

If you are familiar with Regular Expressions you can use them in Contains Letters to limit your search.

A list of suggested words is displayed when you press submit or press the Enter key. All entered data is cleared when the clear button is pressed. The application can be closed with the Escape button. The suggested words are displayed first by highest value first and then if the values are the same, by alphabetical order.

ScrabbleWords has a limited ability to detect whether a word is a bingo (with an extra 50 points). Specifically, it may fail to catch bingos if Contains Letters contains certain regular expressions. This means it is possible (but not likely) that a bingo word may not display first in the suggested word list.

Available Letters

Top

Enter the letters of the tiles in your hand. If you have a blank tile, enter this as a dot (.). Currently, the program cannot handle the situation where the player has both black tiles.

Contains Letters

Top

Enter letters on the board that the suggested word will intersect. Regular expressions are allowed. The regex will be tested to make sure it is a valid Java regular expression.

Starts With

Top

Enter the letter or letters the suggested words should start with. (This is faster than using "^" in a regular expression.)

Ends With

Top

Enter the letter or letters the suggested words should end with. (This is faster than using "$" in a regular expression.)

Dictionary

Top

Select the dictionary to use. The default is OSPD.
